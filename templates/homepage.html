Project Overview

SoundDash provides a comprehensive solution for monitoring urban sound events, helping smart city control centers make informed decisions based on real-time acoustic data.

How It Works

Our network of sensors detects sound events throughout the city. The data is collected and presented on an intuitive dashboard for immediate analysis and action.
